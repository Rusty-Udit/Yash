Becoming

A lens shaped bycuriosity.

I'm Yash — a visual artist and cinematography student drawn to the quiet tension between stillness and motion. My work moves across drawing, photography, painting, and narrative short films, each discipline informing the others.

Trained in the tradition of cinematic composition, I approach every frame as a question: what does the light reveal when everything else is stripped away? My photography pursues the decisive moment; my paintings chase what the camera cannot hold; my films are the space between both.

Currently based in India, I am applying to MFA and BFA programs at Yale, RISD, Pratt, Parsons, and international institutions, seeking to deepen my practice within a rigorous academic environment. This portfolio is not a finished statement — it is a record of becoming.
